Staff Upload

Upload a CSV exported from Kamar. Expected columns (in any order): Code, Last Name, First Name, Title, Email (School).

How to export Staff.csv from Kamar
  1. In Kamar, go to Reporting then Staff Lists.
  2. Choose Staff List - Basic, select the staff to include, then click OK.
  3. In export options choose Spreadsheet, set format to csv, keep Default Fields and Column Headings ticked, then click OK.
  4. Download the CSV file and then upload it here.
