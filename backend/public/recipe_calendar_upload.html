Recipe Calendar Upload

Upload your year-planner PDF to automatically extract the recipe schedule week by week. Review and correct the data before saving it as bookings.

Step 1 — Upload PDF

Select your Middle school Calendar PDF (the year planner). Only the Practical Lessons row is imported.
