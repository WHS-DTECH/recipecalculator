JSON-LD Recipe Instructions:
1. Preheat an oven to 200˚C (180°C fan forced).

2. Place the spinach into a microwave safe bowl. Cook for 1 minute to wilt. Remove, and when cool enough to handle, chop into pieces.

3. Add the 3/4 of the basil, Parmesan, feta, lemon zest, egg, nutmeg, and salt and pepper, combining well.

4. Lie the pastry onto a lightly-floured bench. Cut 12 x 8cm circles from the pastry sheets and place onto a baking tray.

5. Place tablespoons of mixture into the middle of the pastry circles, leaving a 1cm border. Place a tomato into the centre of each. Bake for 20 minutes until puffed and golden.

6. Serve warm with a sprinkle of basil leaves.

Tip: Frozen spinach works well if you don't have fresh. Follow the instructions on the packet to cook.

Spinach & Cheese Puff Tarts

Preparation time

15 mins

Cook time

20 mins
Serves 12
Vegetarian
Low Sugar

Spinach & Cheese Puff Tarts are perfect for getting the kids involved in the kitchen – and they'll also love eating them! This easy puff pastry recipe is out of the oven and in mouths in no time.

Save
Print
Share
Ingredients
Spinach - 120 g
Basil leaves, shredded - 1/2 cup
Cheddar cheese, grated - 3/4 cup
Feta, crumbled - 50 g
Lemon zest
Pinch of nutmeg
Small egg - 1
Salt and pepper, to season
Puff pastry - 2 sheets
Cherry tomatoes - 12

Method

Preheat an oven to 200˚C (180°C fan forced).

Place the spinach into a microwave safe bowl. Cook for 1 minute to wilt. Remove, and when cool enough to handle, chop into pieces.

Add the 3/4 of the basil, Parmesan, feta, lemon zest, egg, nutmeg, and salt and pepper, combining well.

Lie the pastry onto a lightly-floured bench. Cut 12 x 8cm circles from the pastry sheets and place onto a baking tray.

Place tablespoons of mixture into the middle of the pastry circles, leaving a 1cm border. Place a tomato into the centre of each. Bake for 20 minutes until puffed and golden.

Serve warm with a sprinkle of basil leaves.

Tip: Frozen spinach works well if you don't have fresh. Follow the instructions on the packet to cook.
