Method
Heat cooking oil in a large pan. Once the pan is searing hot, fry off the beef until it is nicely browned in colour.
Add onions, potato, kūmara, celery, carrot, and barley.
Sautee vegetables for about 10 minutes.
Add mushrooms, silverbeet, thyme, rosemary and garlic and fry for a further 5 minutes.
Once the meat and vegetables have had time to develop flavour, add wet ingredients; stock, Worcestershire sauce, tomato paste, and season if required.
If the water line is below the meat and vegetables, top up with water so it is the same level as other ingredients.
Cook on low for about 1.5 - 2 hours.
Once complete, cool off for about 20 - 30 minutes before serving.
