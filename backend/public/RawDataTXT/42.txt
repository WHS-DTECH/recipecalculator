JSON-LD Recipe Instructions:
Coat the beef strips in a little oil. Season with salt and pepper.
Preheat a non-stick pan to a moderate heat.
Cook the beef in 3 batches. Cook one side until the first sign of moisture appears on the uncooked side, then turn and cook the other side.
Remove each batch and set aside.
Melt butter in the pan, add the onion and cook until soft.
Add the mushrooms, cook until tender.
Add beef stock and tomato paste, stir to combine and bring to the boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 2 minutes.
Stir in the combined sour cream and cornflour.
Stir well, simmer for 1 minute.
Return beef to pan and simmer for 2 minutes.
Serve with noodles or pasta, and either salad or seasonal vegetables.

Visible Page Text:
Easy beef stroganoff
Quick and easy family favourite

Serves

4

Prep Time

5 mins

Cook Time

20 mins

A good beef stroganoff is all about balance – tender strips of beef, a smooth, creamy sauce, and just the right touch of tang from mustard and sour cream. It’s a dish that comes together quickly, and once you’ve got the sauce just right, you’ve got yourself a meal everyone will enjoy. A quick and easy family meal, we love this with something green on the side - broccolini or beans is perfect.

Allyson Gofton

Ingredients

600g Quality Mark beef steaksi

sliced into strips

30g butter

1 onion

chopped

250g button mushrooms

sliced

3/4 cup beef stock

2 Tbsp tomato paste

250ml sour cream

1 tsp cornflour

To serve

pasta

Method
1

Coat the beef strips in a little oil. Season with salt and pepper.

2

Preheat a non-stick pan to a moderate heat.

3

Cook the beef in 3 batches. Cook one side until the first sign of moisture appears on the uncooked side, then turn and cook the other side.

4

Remove each batch and set aside.

5

Melt butter in the pan, add the onion and cook until soft.

6

Add the mushrooms, cook until tender.

7

Add beef stock and tomato paste, stir to combine and bring to the boil.

8

Reduce heat and simmer for 2 minutes.

9

Stir in the combined sour cream and cornflour.

10

Stir well, simmer for 1 minute.

11

Return beef to pan and simmer for 2 minutes.

12

Serve with noodles or pasta, and either salad or seasonal vegetables.
