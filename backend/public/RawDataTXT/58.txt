Method
Get a large cooking surface searing hot then fry off mince, onion, carrot and celery until nicely coloured.
Add mushrooms, silverbeet and garlic and cook down for 3-4 minutes.
Once cooked, add tinned tomato, rosemary and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. This completes the meat sauce.
In a large soup pot, heat a teaspoon of oil, then add butter and season.
Once butter is melted and lightly browned, add nutmeg and flour. Cook flour for 2-3 minutes. Pour in milk and bring to a slight simmer.
Reserving some cheese for the top of the lasagne, add cheese, and allow the sauce to thicken. This completes the cheese sauce.
Once both sauces are complete, build the lasagne within large oven trays. Start with a little meat sauce on the bottom to stop the pasta from sticking or burning. Then, cover with sheets of pasta.
Lay meat sauce on top of pasta sheets, spread cheese sauce over the meat sauce, then layer pasta over top.
Repeat this process for your specifications. The top layer should be meat sauce then cheese, to finish and create a crust.
Place in oven preheated to 180 degrees and cook until pasta sheets are soft.
