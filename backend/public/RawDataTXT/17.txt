Spaghetti & Cheese Toastie

Preparation time

10 mins

Cook time

5 mins
Serves 2
Save
Print
Share
Ingredients
Bread, sliced thickly - 4 pieces
Butter - 2 tsp
Tinned spaghetti - 1/2 cup
Colby cheese, grated - 1/2 cup
Add items to trolley
Method

Preheat a sandwich maker.

Butter one side of each slice of bread. Place the butter side down on the hot plate.

Top with 1⁄4 cup of spaghetti and 1⁄4 cup of Colby cheese, then add the second slice of bread butter side up.

Cook for 4 minutes. Let them sit for a couple of minutes before slicing them in half.

Alternatively:

Cook in a preheated frying pan to medium heat, turning after 3 minutes for a further 2-3 minutes.
