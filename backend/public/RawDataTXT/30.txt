JSON-LD Recipe Instructions:
1. In a small pan, place the garlic and anchovies and cook for 3-4 minutes. Add the cream and bring to a simmer for a further 2 minutes until thickened.

2. Remove from the heat and add the lemon juice. 

3. Season with salt and pepper and set aside to cool. 

4. Rub the asparagus with olive oil and fry in a pan at medium heat for 5-6 minutes, or until slightly charred. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.

5. Place wedges of lettuce in a bowl and toss through the dressing, coating evenly. 

6. Add the drained tuna and shaved parmesan, gently combining, and season to taste.

7. To poach the eggs, half fill a frying pan with water, bringing to a simmer. Break in the eggs and poach to your liking.

8. Place the salad onto four plates. Top each with a poached egg, asparagus and some croutons and enjoy!

Tip: 
- To make croutons, toss 1 cup of bread cubes in a bowl with 1 tablespoon of oil. Bake in a frying pan or oven for 8 minutes until crispy. 
- The dressing will last in the fridge for 2-3 days.

Asparagus & Tuna Caesar Salad

Preparation time

20 mins

Cook time

20 mins
Serves 4
Low Sugar

You’ll forget all about the humble green side with our recipe for Asparagus & Tuna Caesar Salad. This easy and delicious dish is perfect for when you’re entertaining or need to take a plate.

Save
Print
Share
Ingredients
Clove garlic, crushed - 1
Anchovies, chopped finely - 2
Cream - 1/2 cup
Lemon juice - 2 Tbsp
Salt and pepper, to taste
Olive oil - 1 Tbsp
Asparagus, trimmed - 1 bunch
Baby cos lettuce, cut into wedges - 2
Tinned tuna in spring water, drained - 450 g
Shaved parmesan - 1/2 cup
Eggs - 4
Croutons - 1 cup

Method

In a small pan, place the garlic and anchovies and cook for 3-4 minutes. Add the cream and bring to a simmer for a further 2 minutes until thickened.

Remove from the heat and add the lemon juice.

Season with salt and pepper and set aside to cool.

Rub the asparagus with olive oil and fry in a pan at medium heat for 5-6 minutes, or until slightly charred. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.

Place wedges of lettuce in a bowl and toss through the dressing, coating evenly.

Add the drained tuna and shaved parmesan, gently combining, and season to taste.

To poach the eggs, half fill a frying pan with water, bringing to a simmer. Break in the eggs and poach to your liking.

Place the salad onto four plates. Top each with a poached egg, asparagus and some croutons and enjoy!

Tip:

To make croutons, toss 1 cup of bread cubes in a bowl with 1 tablespoon of oil. Bake in a frying pan or oven for 8 minutes until crispy.
The dressing will last in the fridge for 2-3 days.
