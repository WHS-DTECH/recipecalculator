Method
Cook rice according to instructions.
Sauté onions, carrots and parsnips over medium heat until soft.
Add garlic and ginger and sauté until fragrant (about 1 minute).
Add mince and brown on medium to high heat.
Once mince is browned, add sesame oil, teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce and brown sugar. Simmer for 15 minutes.
While mince is simmering, lightly toast the sesame seeds and set aside.
Add spinach to the mince mixture, cover and gently simmer for 5 minutes until wilted then stir through evenly.
Serve immediately on a bed of rice with a sprinkling of sesame seeds to garnish.

Cook’s kōrero

This quick and simple meal is full of flavour and a top pick for our tamariki. I love how easy it is to pack vegetables into the beef mixture. We can include about 100g of cooked vegetables per serve for our year 4-8 tamariki, with them still loving the meal.
